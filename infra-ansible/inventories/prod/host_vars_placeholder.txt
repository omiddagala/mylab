Create host_vars/<hostname>.yml files from your IPAM-generated data.
This repository expects exact addressing and interface maps to come from the mylab IPAM repo.
Below, the roles already work with host vars such as:
- mgmt_interface
- mgmt_ip_cidr
- mgmt_gateway
- leaf_vlans
- leaf_svis
- frr_router_id
- frr_bgp_asn
- frr_bgp_neighbors
- k8s_node_labels
